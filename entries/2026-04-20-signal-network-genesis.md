A 22-agent mesh spanning my companies:

- **KULN** — streetwear brand (editorial minimalism, Shopify, drop pipeline)
- **LIMBO** — fan community (3,851 verified members, Discord ecosystem, subscription)
- **1Z Entertainment** — self-managed company for SB19 (merch.1zentertainment.ph)
